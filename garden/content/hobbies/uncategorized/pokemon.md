## Video Games

New pokemon games were usually released in the Fall, so every year, I would ask for the new games for Christmas. I was lucky enough to have generous Aunts and Uncles, so I would often get both games of that generation.

One of my [[side quests]] is to complete all mainline pokemon games.

## Trading Cards

I started collecting pokemon cards in 2024, which unfortunately was at the peak of scalpers. As of now, I don't really have a goal or collection target. I just like finding really cool art or funny looking cards.
