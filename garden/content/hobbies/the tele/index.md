Growing up as a kid, I would watch cartoons on tv everyday in the morning. I grew up on Nickelodeon, Cartoon Network, and Disney Channel shows; Spongebob being my favorite amongst them.

Now that streaming exists, it feels like cable television is dying out. It's so easy to just pick any episode and watch it from your phone, tablet, or computer from nearly anywhere in the world.

Streaming is super convenient, but it's a bit sad in a way. I have fond memories of watching Survivor with my family at 8pm every Wednesday or The Amazing Race on Sundays. Sometimes, we'd have to set up the DVR to record the episode and then we'd watch it on the weekend so we wouldn't be confused for the next episode.
